Plan of Attack

The following is the plan of attack I plan to (sort of) follow - at the very least it's a list of topics that I want to touch upon in relation to EPUB

  • EPUB 3 file format (.epub)
  • SVG in EPUB
    • Graphviz dot supports output to svg
  • epub:type and its attributes. A good reference would be: http://idpf.org/epub/vocab/structure/
  • MathML in EPUB
  • LaTeX conversion to EPUB (probably via HTML)
