This is my introductory text on EPUB (3) written entirely from scratch, and zipped manually. Why would anyone want to do this? Well, first of all, I want to understand the EPUB format. Secondly, I can't find any good texts on EPUB(3), and most editing programs freely available only speak EPUB 2.

I'm also slightly afraid that my Sony Reader(TM) does not get EPUB 3, although I know for a fact this it supports SVG, but I'm not entirely sure on MathML (which I'd be really interested in).
